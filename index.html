Welcome to About a Guitar!

The ultimate source for all things Kurt Cobain and his guitars.

Welcome to Kurt Cobain Guitars, the go-to destination for everything related to Kurt Cobain's guitars. Our website is dedicated to providing you with comprehensive information on each of the guitars he used, their history, and any modifications made to them.
We have compiled an extensive database of Kurt Cobain's guitars, including detailed specs, photos, and even video demonstrations. You can explore each model he used, from the humble Fender Mustang to the iconic Fender Jaguar and the Gibson SG. You'll also learn about the unique modifications he made to each guitar to achieve his signature sound.
Whether you're a guitar collector, a Nirvana fan, or simply a music lover, we invite you to explore our website and discover the rich history and legacy of Kurt Cobain's guitars.
